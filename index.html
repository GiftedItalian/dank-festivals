Facilities

Food

There will be a variety of different foods available within each locations arena. Due to safety reasons, no food from outside the festival can be taken inside the main site but we provide plenty of bars and food stands. Each food product is not only very well priced but also of great quality.

Car Parking

We provide plenty of parking spaces at each venue. There is a £5 charge which needs to be payed at arrival for a parking spot. Disabled parking spots are also available which are free. If you own a VIP ticket then parking is included.

Toilets / Baby changing

There will be dozens of toilets spread in and around the arena with baby changing facilities available. The better the user treats the user, then the cleaner the toilets will be.

Fire / First aid / Security

Fire escapes will be clearly labelled to ensure optimal safety for the customers. Numerous Security guards and First Aiders will be available all over the arena which can assist you if the need arises.

Special needs / Disabled access

If any of your group has special needs or a disability, we would recommend you notify us before attending the event in order to make necassary arrangements.

Customers who have purchased a disabled ticket can bring a guest or a personal assistant with them free of charge.
